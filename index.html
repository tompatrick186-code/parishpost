The Thornbury Village Post
March 2026 · Your monthly community update

Parish Council News

The council has approved the long-awaited resurfacing of Church Lane, with work scheduled to begin in April. Residents on the lane will receive a letter with full details of the temporary road closure ahead of the start date.

Planning permission has been granted for the new community noticeboard at the village green. The Friends of Thornbury have kindly offered to fund the installation.

Upcoming Events

Sat 22 March — Spring Quiz Night, The Crown Inn, 7pm. Teams of up to six, £3 per person.
Sun 30 March — Easter Egg Hunt, Village Green, 10am–12pm. Free entry for under 12s.
Fri 4 April — Parish Council Meeting, Village Hall, 7:30pm. All welcome.

Community Notices

The village litter pick will take place on Saturday 5 April, meeting at the car park at 9am. Bags and gloves provided. All welcome.
